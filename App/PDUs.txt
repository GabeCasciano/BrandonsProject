PDUs:

I - Identified
    - ID <int>; Name <string>

P - Persons Info
    - ID <int>; Name <string> (both are optional, but atleast 1 is required, position is important)

Note: this will be initially sent from the client to the server, the server will then respond with
the following sequence of PDUs

O - Organization Info
    - Num of People <int>

    and then a series of P PDUs

A - Ack

E - Error
    - error_code <int>

    1. could not find person with matching ID
    2. could not find person with matching name
    3.
